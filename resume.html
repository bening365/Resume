Beach Conservation, Hawaii>

"It all comes down to where you stand and the view you want to see"

Beach Conservationclose

    I always liked nature. Growing up climbing cherry trees, hoping that there were more cherries to share. I guess climbing trees wasn't an option anymore when it came to adulthood. I wonder if that tree is still there, waiting for kids to drop their bike and rush like it was time for dinner.

   I am glad I was that kid. It wasn't the taste of the cherry that I miss. It was the view and the relief that came with it. A memory that is imprinted in my mind when I saw the sunlight, glistening on my friend’s face as they yelled: "on the top left, not my left, your left!”. This memory has taught me everything is beautiful, I just have to see it from a different view.

    My love for nature brought me to Hawaii. It was mesmerizing to see how most of the "sand" particles were plastic. The fact that so many of our everyday products ended up in the oceans was heart-breaking. I can give you a smile, but I can't give you the feeling of what mother nature has suffered.

General Assembly, NEW YORK>

Creating a dating website

Huffien.Clubclose

    Creating a website had been on my mind for some time. So, I set a goal to put aside a percentage of my money from my past and current internships to pay for a course to take a front-end development class during summer 2019 at General Assembly in NYC.

    Setting that as a goal and taking the course were the best decisions I made! It helped me understand the basics of HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT and DOM. For my final project, I created an entertainment website called Huffien Club.

  Huffien.Club is a parody of dating app. I used dogs as my target audience to communicate and get my goal across. I understand, as learned during my research, people download dating apps because they want real connections. Each dating app has its own unique selling point. Hinge was my favorite example of a dating app because it, according to their advertising campaign, is designed to be deleted. Huffien.club is the opposite of Hinge; it wants you to join the club and have fun without feelings attached.

    *Don’t you wish you don’t catch feelings too? I had so much fun creating the website. I hope this website gives you a smile, if not you are one more Dating apps richer :)
